Report Turnaround Times

I understand the importance of timely delivery and aim to work to your deadlines wherever possible. Estimated turnaround times from receipt of full documentation are as follows:

  • Screening Reports: 4 weeks
  • CPR 35 Compliant Full Reports: 10-12 weeks (depending on complexity and volume of records)
  • NHS Resolution ENS Reports: As agreed (typically within tight deadlines set by the scheme)
  • Professional Regulation Reports (NMC): 10-12 weeks (depending on complexity and volume of records)
  • Supplementary Reports: 4 weeks

Urgent Instructions: I may be able to accommodate urgent instructions subject to availability and may charge an expedited fee. Please discuss urgent timelines at the point of initial enquiry.
