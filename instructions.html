Instructions & Fees

Transparent fee structure and clear instruction process for solicitors.

Instruction Process

To formally instruct me or to check my availability and conduct a conflict check, please contact me via email or telephone. I aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours.

Step 1: Initial Enquiry

Please provide:

  • Names of all parties involved
  • Date of the event
  • NHS Trust and hospital involved
  • Claimant, Defendant, or Joint instruction
  • Report type required

I will confirm availability and conduct a conflict check immediately.

Step 2: Formal Instruction

Once confirmed, please send:

  • Letter of instruction with questions
  • Medical records (paginated/indexed)
  • Relevant policies/guidelines
  • Witness statements & other expert reports

I will confirm acceptance and provide a formal fee and delivery timescale.

Fees & Terms
