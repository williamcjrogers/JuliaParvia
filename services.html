Medico-Legal Reporting Services

1. CPR 35 Court Compliant Full Reports

Comprehensive expert witness reports fully compliant with Part 35 of the Civil Procedure Rules. Prepared for clinical negligence litigation, including:

  • Detailed chronological analysis of clinical care
  • Assessment of adherence to standards (NICE, RCOG, RCM) and trust policies
  • Expert opinion on breach of duty, liability, and causation
  • Clear, evidence-based reasoning supporting all conclusions

2. Limited Screening Advisory Reports

Preliminary reports to assist solicitors in early case assessment. Provides:

  • Initial expert opinion on potential merits of a claim
  • Identification of key clinical issues requiring investigation
  • Cost-effective early case evaluation

3. Early Notification NHS Resolution Reports

Expert reports under the NHS Resolution Early Notification Scheme (ENS). Prepared to tight timescales with rapid, thorough analysis of breach of duty and causation.

4. Professional Regulation Reports (NMC)

Expert reports for professional regulation and Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC) hearings. Extensive experience with specific templates and the hearing process.

Statement of Impartiality

I understand my overriding duty is to the Court, as stipulated by CPR 35. I accept instructions from Claimants, Defendants, and on a Joint basis.
