Initial Enquiry Information

To enable me to conduct a conflict check and provide an accurate estimate, please include the following information in your initial enquiry:

  • Your name, organization, and contact details
  • Whether you are instructing on behalf of the Claimant, Defendant, or jointly
  • The names of all parties involved in the case
  • The NHS Trust or hospital where care was provided
  • The approximate dates of the incident
  • A brief summary of the key clinical issues
  • The type of report required (screening, full CPR 35, or NHS Resolution ENS)
  • Any specific deadlines or urgent requirements
